CodeLaTeX

An AI-powered LaTeX editor being built for the research lab — a desktop application combining a Monaco editor, live PDF preview with SyncTeX, and an AI sidebar (proof-checking, fixes, and chat modes) backed by a local model router and a retrieval pipeline over the lab's papers and notes.

Stack: Tauri 2.x · Monaco Editor · PDF.js · FastAPI · Ollama · ChromaDB
